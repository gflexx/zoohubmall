Product Names, Attributes and Keywords
  1. Product names must be consistent with the other product-related information, including product photos, product descriptions, etc. They must be as accurate, complete and concise as possible. Any Users may use product names, generic names and etc
  2. Product attributes must be consistent with the actual condition of the product, and accurately showing the ingredients, materials, sizes, brands, models, places of origin, etc
  3. Users may set 1-3 keywords that match the products to facilitate buyersâ€™ searches
Product Description
  1. Any brief and detailed descriptions of a product shall be based on the actual condition of the product. Such descriptions can introduce functions, characteristics, specific instructions for use, packaging information and accessories of the product in detail, and display pictures of products, product details and packaging as well as the design sketches, etc
Product Images and videos
  1. Product images must be consistent with the text information and must accurately reflect the actual situation of the product. Unless otherwise specified, images & videos must be clear, complete, without smear or cover.
  2. Image specifications: the size of the image must not exceed 3MB; the image ratio must be between 1:1 and 1:1.3 and the resolution must be greater than 350px * 350px. Images that do not meet the specifications will not be uploaded to the product release interface
Payment and Delivery
  1. Payment and delivery options must be consistent with the actual service capability of suppliers
