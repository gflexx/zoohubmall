Help & Support


  • Site Navigation

    BAG represents your cart, add items to your Bag then proceed to checkout.

    The STORES page shows all stores that are open in the mall.

    In CATEGORIES you can view our vast variety of product categories.

    In the Accounts page, you can check your details, update your password and profile.

    The Dashboard is where sellers can manage their store and products.

    View your order history at the Orders page.

  • How to Manage Your Store

    Update and delete your products at the All Products page.

    Check all your customer orders in the Orders page.

    The Stock page shows your products' quantities, quantities in red show low stock.

    The Metrics page shows graphs representing statistics about your store performance.

  • Product Delivery

    You can only collect products at your chosen specified area.

    Delivery within Nairobi and its whereabouts is done within 12 hours of ordering.

    Delivery outside Nairobi is done within 36 hours of ordering.

    Upon delivery, the customer is given a form to fill for delivery confirmation.

    Delivery cost will depend on the nature of the product and distant to your location from our HQ but with a minimum fee of Kshs. 50.

  • Return Policy

    Returned products can only be accepted within 72 hours of order.

    The buyer is responsible for the return cost.

    Returned products can only to be returned to any of our specified outlet offices.

    Perishable products cannot be returned.
